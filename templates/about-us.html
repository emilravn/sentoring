What is Sentoring?

Sentoring is a tool that will retrieve and analyze tweets made by users on Twitter based on the word(s) given to the query. These tweets will then be fed into VADER, a tool for conducting sentimental analysis, recognizing positive, negative, and neutral sentiment in this online text data. Sentoring will display the results of the analysis through statistical models models and show the sentiment perceived by Twitter users based on tweets collected in the past week.

This tool was made by three BSc. students in Information Technology at Aalborg University in 2021.
