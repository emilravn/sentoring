What is Sentoring?

Sentoring is a tool that will retrieve and analyze statements said by individuals on Twitter. These statements will then be fed into a sentimental model for further analysis, recognizing positive, negative, annd neutral emotions in online text data. Ultimately, it will tell and show you through statistical models whether what you searched for is in good standing with the general public.

By analyzing the sentiment in social media comments, businesses can better understand how customers feel about their brands and prouducts.

This tool was made by three BSc. students in Information Technology at Aalborg University in 2021.
